| T-04-06 | Tampering | Two processes racing SetBucketLifecycle at boot | accept | `SetBucketLifecycle` is an idempotent full-document PUT; the API is the single designated owner (Pitfall 4) so no race exists in practice; documented, not defended with a lock. |
| T-04-07 | Denial of Service | Sub-day STORAGE_TTL silently becoming a 0-day rule | mitigate | `lifecycleConfig` clamps `days` up to a minimum of 1 (Pitfall 3); a 0-day/invalid rule can never be emitted. |
