<objective>
Add the data-layer half of RECON-04: a `FindWebhookGaps` finder that detects `done`/`failed` jobs whose completion webhook was silently never enqueued (zero `webhook_deliveries` rows), a `RecordWebhookGapRecovered` event-writer, and a supporting non-partial index.

Purpose: The reconciler sweep (Plan 03) needs a query that reliably distinguishes "enqueue never happened at all" (a genuine gap, per D-04) from "delivery was attempted and exhausted/dead-lettered" (never re-swept, per D-05). A `NOT EXISTS` anti-join against ALL `webhook_deliveries` rows is what makes dead-lettered jobs correctly excluded.

Output: migration `0004_webhook_deliveries_job_idx.sql`, `WebhookGapJob`/`FindWebhookGaps`/`RecordWebhookGapRecovered`/`detailActionWebhookGapRecovered` in `repo.go`, and `TestFindWebhookGaps`.
