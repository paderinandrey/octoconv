<objective>
Make the reconciler sweep engine-aware. Today it hardcodes every stranded-job recovery onto the image queue (`EnqueueImageConvert` at reconciler.go:127) — a launch-blocking gap for documents. This plan adds an `Engine` field to `StaleJob` (populated from the already-existing `jobs.engine` column, no migration), extends the reconciler's `enqueuer` interface with `EnqueueDocumentConvert`, and routes recovery by engine with a fail-closed default.

Purpose: DOC-09 — a stranded document job must be recovered through the document queue, never misrouted onto the image queue. The security requirement adds: an unrecognized/corrupted engine value must be a clear, non-fatal, metric-visible skip (fail-closed), never a silent misroute or a sweep-loop crash.

Output: Engine-aware stale-job recovery with regression-safe image routing, correct document routing, and a fail-closed skip for out-of-scope engines.
</objective>
