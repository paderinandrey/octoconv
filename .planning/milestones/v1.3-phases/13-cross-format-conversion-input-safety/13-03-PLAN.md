| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-13-07 | Tampering | committed CFB fixtures | accept | legacy.doc / encrypted.docx are inert test data asserted only for 422 rejection; they are never converted, never reach the worker, and carry no macro/exec payload the pipeline would run. |
