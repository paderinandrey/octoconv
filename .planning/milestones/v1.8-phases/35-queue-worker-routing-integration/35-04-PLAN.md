<objective>
Implement D-07's two-tier upload ceiling, add `EnqueueAVConvert` to the API's `Enqueuer` seam with its routing case, and close the phase's highest-risk silent seam by rewiring the queue-depth collector to `queue.AllConvertQueues()`.

Purpose: Video files are legitimately larger than any other engine class's inputs. Raising the single global ceiling alone would silently weaken the DoS posture of four working engine classes; the per-engine post-detection check is what makes the raise safe.
Output: A 2 GiB global cap, per-engine ceilings holding image/document/html/audio at 100 MiB, an av-capable `Enqueuer`, and a derived collector list.
</objective>
