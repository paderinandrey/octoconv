<objective>
Build the stdlib-only container-inspection detection layer that disambiguates the six ZIP-based office formats and gathers the zip-bomb and macro-part safety signals â€” plus the small predicate that fixes the confirmed dimension-check regression. This is the pure-logic tier of Phase 8; the handler that consumes these functions is Plan 08-02.

Purpose: All six office formats share the identical `PK\x03\x04` magic bytes, so `sniff.go`'s prefix-match table cannot tell them apart (D-02). Disambiguation requires reading the ZIP central directory via `archive/zip`, which the existing `Sniff` signature (`io.Reader`) cannot support. This plan delivers the new detection contract that Plan 08-02 wires into `handleCreateJob`.

Output: `internal/convert/docsniff.go` (new), `internal/convert/docsniff_test.go` (new), and an additive `HasDimensionLimit` predicate in `internal/convert/dimensions.go` with a test.
</objective>
