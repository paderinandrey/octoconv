<objective>
Close two independent, well-diagnosed v1.3 tail-debt items:

- DEBT-06: remove the dead webhook wiring (`webhook.NewRepo`/`webhook.NewDeliverer` + `WEBHOOK_SIGNING_SECRET` read + the `internal/webhook` import) from `cmd/document-worker` and `cmd/chromium-worker`. Purpose: unblock the v1.4 CI `-race` tier (DEBT-07) and remove a stale copy of the HMAC signing secret from two process footprints (DEBT-06).
Output: two worker binaries with no webhook coupling; a race-safe reconciler test helper.
</objective>
