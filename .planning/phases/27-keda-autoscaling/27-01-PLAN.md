The ShutdownTimeout fix removes a confirmed Phase-28 blocker (asynq's 8s default silently caps every worker's grace window regardless of pod terminationGracePeriodSeconds).

Output: Pure-Go change to 5 `cmd/*/main.go` files, one extended unit test, one new compose-E2E assertion, one validation-only compose override. Zero chart/manifest changes, zero new Go dependencies.
