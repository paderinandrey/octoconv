<objective>
Establish the local dev toolchain for the audio engine and build the two fail-closed input-validation controls that must run before any audio bytes are decoded: (1) a bespoke, ID3v2-aware, variable-offset magic-bytes detector for mp3/wav/m4a/ogg (AUD-01), and (2) an ffprobe-based declared-duration guard (AUD-04, the audio analog of the image decompression-bomb dimension guard).

This plan also provisions the pinned whisper-cli v1.9.1 binary + base model that Plan 03's live tests require.
