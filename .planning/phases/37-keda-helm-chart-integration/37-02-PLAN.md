<decisions>
D-07 (37-CONTEXT.md) requires TWO distinct live proofs, so this plan builds TWO instruments:
1. Scale-from-zero (SC3): enqueue an av backlog at 0 replicas → KEDA resolves it via the always-on api collector → scales 0→1→…→N → drains → back to 0. Timestamped kubectl/Prometheus evidence.
2. Downscale survival (SC4, load-bearing): with an in-flight LONG av transcode, trigger a genuine N→N-1 HPA downscale and prove the job completes gracefully (no exit-137/SIGKILL mid-job) — validates terminationGracePeriodSeconds=783 live.

Fixture strategy (av-specific divergence from audio): av has NO committed video binary. scripts/av-rtf-measure.sh's precedent is authoritative — synthesize fixtures ENTIRELY in-container via ffmpeg lavfi (testsrc + sine), never commit a large binary. The scale-from-zero fixture is short (a few seconds is enough to trigger a queued job and observe 0→1). The downscale-survival fixture must be LONG enough that the transcode is still in-flight when the N→N-1 downscale fires — synthesize a fixture near the AV_MAX_DURATION_SECONDS=90 ceiling so the worst-cell transcode (hevc@1080, p95 RTF≈4.18) runs long (~hundreds of seconds), comfortably outliving the 15s observable-downscale window while staying under the 783s production grace.

Observable-downscale override (mirrors the document precedent already in values-loadproof.yaml): production keda.av.scaleDownStabilizationSeconds is 900s (Plan 01), which would push the N→N-1 downscale far past any reasonable gate window. Add keda.av.scaleDownStabilizationSeconds:15 to values-loadproof.yaml so the downscale fires promptly and is observable — while the Deployment's terminationGracePeriodSeconds stays at the PRODUCTION 783s (that is exactly the value being validated; do NOT lower it in any overlay). Layer values-loadproof.yaml ON TOP of values-local.yaml, never standalone (same convention as the existing document override).

No CALIBRATE mode (mirrors keda-audio-loadproof.sh): AV_ENGINE_TIMEOUT is a hard committed constant; nothing to calibrate.
Byte-unchanged discipline: do NOT touch keda-load-proof.sh, keda-gate.sh, or keda-audio-loadproof.sh — av logic lives exclusively in the two new files.
</decisions>
